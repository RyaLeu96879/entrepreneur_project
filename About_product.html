About The Project

Human body generates heat, and heat can generates power. We realize that people can't charge their phone outside of their house if they don't bring a charger of any type. Our idea is to use the heat human body generates and use it to charge the phone. We want to make a phonecase that can take in the heat from a person's hand and use it to charge people's phone so people can charge the phone on the go without needing a charger or a portable charger.
